Have questions about how Aljazeera Transits works? Find quick answers regarding our cargo operations and escrow system here.

For Cargo Owners (Shippers)

When you accept a matched transporter's bid, your payment is safely deposited into our network platform hold (escrow). The driver can see that the payment is guaranteed and locked, motivating them to move your cargo quickly. However, the money is only released into the driver's wallet after you confirm safe delivery at the destination coordinates.

Every single carrier and fleet driver on Aljazeera Transits undergoes a mandatory onboarding screen. We strictly verify corporate registration documents, active Goods in Transit (GIT) cargo insurance policies, valid heavy vehicle commercial driver's licenses, and truck logbooks before approving them to bid on jobs.

Our global routing and matching algorithms operate strictly using the International Metric System. Please input your cargo loads using Metric Tons (1 Metric Ton = 1,000 Kilograms). This standard keeps cross-border customs declarations perfectly accurate and avoids accidental truck overloading.

For Transporters & Carriers

Payouts are processed instantly when the cargo owner signs off or confirms successful delivery through their dashboard. Once verified, the escrow system clears the funds and distributes them directly into your secure carrier dashboard account wallet for withdrawal.
