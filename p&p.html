Ecosystem Privacy Policy

Last Updated and Revised: June 19, 2026

1. Information Framework Collection

At Aljazeera Transits, we prioritize processing security and transparency regarding your operational metrics. When using our logistics matching services, we gather data essential for corridor safety and escrow verification:

  • Account Profiling Credentials: Organization names, company registration paperwork, and contact channels for both Shippers and Carriers.
  • Telemetry & Assets Tracking: Real-time GPS location readings of active transporter trucks on cargo routes to confirm milestones.
  • Financial Transaction Logs: Safe escrow funding patterns, billing information, and bank settlement distributions.

2. How We Utilize Marketplace Metrics

Your data is strictly applied to optimize cross-border freight matching channels. Specifically, tracking coordinates help calculate target pickup windows, determine accurate fuel/route balances, and manage automatic contract tranche clearings. We do not monetize or distribute individual route histories or trade values to third-party ad networks.

3. Security Architecture & Escrow Vault Protection

All private corporate records and asset positions are securely locked within fortified technical data arrays. Access to matching transactional metrics is restricted strictly to verified parties linked to that explicit manifest contract, or to system admin profiles running dispute arbitrations.

Encryption and Integrity Standard: Account documentation, cryptographic tokens for milestone releases, and financial records are protected using high-tier Transport Layer Security (TLS) and AES-256 databases.

4. Data Rights & Governance Control

Registered operators hold absolute control over their platform presence. You retain full rights to update registration items, pause active asset broadcasting loops when off-duty, or submit a profile closing log to clear records from the active marketplace cache.
